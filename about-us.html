Little Pascals Engineering is a small engineering firm established in 1951 and based in the heart of Portland in the Goose Hollow area. We have been operated and owned by our employees for the entire duration of our companies existance.

Our firm specializes in both mechanical and structural engineering. We operate both in the public and private sector. Reducing our environemental impact, keeping costs down for our clients, and getting projects done as efficiently as possible are our core tennants.
